NEET PG Counselling Bihar: A Guide to the Admission Process

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level postgraduate medical entrance examination conducted by the National Board of Examinations (NBME) for admission to postgraduate medical courses in India. NEET PG is a single window exam for admission to MD/MS/Diploma/DM/MCh programs in government, private, deemed universities, and private medical colleges in India.

Bihar is one of the states in India that offers postgraduate medical education. There are a number of government and private medical colleges in Bihar that offer postgraduate medical programs. The cut-offs for NEET PG counselling in Bihar are generally lower than the national cut-offs.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for NEET PG counselling in Bihar, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • They must have passed the MBBS/BDS examination from a recognized university.
  • They must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate (40% for SC/ST candidates and 45% for PwD candidates).
  • They must have completed 1 year of internship as a medical graduate.
  • They must have a valid NEET PG score.

Application Process

The application process for NEET PG counselling in Bihar is conducted online through the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) website. The application form for NEET PG 2023 will be available on the BCECEB website from March 1 to April 30, 2023.

The application fee for NEET PG counselling in Bihar is INR 1,800 for General/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST candidates. The application fee can be paid online using Debit/Credit/Net banking.

Merit List

The merit list for NEET PG counselling in Bihar will be prepared on the basis of the candidate's NEET PG score and the choice of specialization. The merit list will be published on the BCECEB website after the NEET PG exam is conducted.

Seat Allotment

The seat allotment for NEET PG counselling in Bihar will be done through the BCECEB online counselling process. The candidates will be allotted a seat in a postgraduate medical program in a medical college in Bihar based on their NEET PG score, choice of specialization, and category.

Important Dates

  • NEET PG 2023 exam: September 9, 2023
  • NEET PG 2023 result: October 20, 2023
  • NEET PG counselling Bihar 2023: November 2023

Conclusion

NEET PG counselling in Bihar is a transparent and fair way to allocate seats in postgraduate medical programs in medical colleges in Bihar. However, due to the relatively low number of postgraduate medical seats in Bihar, candidates may have to compromise on their choice of specialization or may have to travel to other states to pursue their postgraduate medical education.

Additional Information

  • The number of postgraduate medical seats in Bihar is around 500.

  • The BCECEB counselling process for Bihar is divided into two rounds. The first round is for the state quota seats, and the second round is for the All India Quota (AIQ) seats.

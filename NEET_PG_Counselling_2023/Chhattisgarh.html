Chhattisgarh NEET PG Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to MD/MS/Diploma/DM/MCh programs in government, private, deemed universities, and private medical schools in India, NEET PG is a single window test.

 

 

One of the Indian states that provides postgraduate medical education is Chhattisgarh. In Chhattisgarh, there are several public and private medical schools that provide postgraduate medical training. In Chhattisgarh, the cut-offs for NEET PG counseling are often lower than the national cut-offs.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Chhattisgarh's NEET PG counseling:

 

They must have succeeded on the MBBS/BDS test from an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

They need a legitimate NEET PG score.

Application Method

 

The Directorate of Medical Education, Chhattisgarh (DMET) website is where applicants for NEET PG counseling in that state submit their applications. From March 1 through April 30, 2023, the DMET website will host the NEET PG 2023 application form.

 

For Chhattisgarh's NEET PG counseling, the application fee is INR 1,800 for general/OBC applicants and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG score and selected speciality will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG counseling in Chhattisgarh. After the NEET PG test is completed, the merit list will be made available on the DMET website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The DMET online counseling mechanism will be used to provide seats for Chhattisgarh's NEET PG counseling session. Based on the students' NEET PG scores, specialty preferences, and category, seats in postgraduate medical programs at medical colleges in Chhattisgarh will be assigned.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for the 2023 NEET PG: September 9

Result of NEET PG 2023: October 20, 2023

Counseling for NEET PG in Chhattisgarh November of 2023

Conclusion

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate medical programs in Chhattisgarh's medical institutions is via NEET PG counseling. To continue their postgraduate medical education, students may have to make compromises on their chosen expertise or go to other states owing to Chhattisgarh's comparatively low number of postgraduate medical seats.

 

Additional Details

 

In Chhattisgarh, there are around 418 postgraduate medical seats available.

The threshold for Chhattisgarh's NEET PG counseling fluctuates from year to year. But 600 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

Two rounds make up the DMET counseling procedure in Chhattisgarh. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Nagaland NEET PG Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programmes in India. For admission to MD/MS/Diploma/DM/MCh programmes in government, private, deemed universities, and private medical schools in India, NEET PG is a single window test.

 

 

One of the Indian states that provides postgraduate medical education is Nagaland. Nagaland Medical College (NMC) is the only government-run medical school in the state. In a number of specialisations, NMC provides postgraduate medical courses.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfil the following requirements in order to be eligible for NEET PG counselling in Nagaland:

 

They must have succeeded on the MBBS/BDS test from an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

They need a legitimate NEET PG score.

Application Method

 

The Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Nagaland website is used to submit applications for NEET PG counselling in Nagaland. From March 1 through April 30, 2023, the NEET PG application form will be accessible on the website of the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare.

 

For NEET PG counselling in Nagaland, the application fee is INR 1,800 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG score and selected specialisation will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG counselling in Nagaland. Following the NEET PG test, the merit list will be posted on the website of the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Health and Family Welfare's online counselling mechanism will be used to provide seats for NEET PG counselling in Nagaland. Based on the applicants' NEET PG scores, specialisation preferences, and category, NMC will assign them a place in a postgraduate medical programme.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for the 2023 NEET PG: September 9

Result of NEET PG 2023: October 20, 2023

Nagaland NEET PG counselling November of 2023

Conclusion

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate medical programmes at NMC is via NEET PG counselling in Nagaland. To continue their postgraduate medical education, students may have to make compromises on their chosen specialisation or go to neighbouring states owing to the restricted number of postgraduate medical seats in Nagaland.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 250 postgraduate medical seats are available in Nagaland.

The cut-off for Nagaland's NEET PG counselling changes every year. But 600 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two phases to the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare's counselling procedure for Nagaland. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

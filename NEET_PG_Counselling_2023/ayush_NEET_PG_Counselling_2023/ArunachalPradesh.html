Arunachal Pradesh's NEET PG Ayush Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

Counseling for Arunachal Pradesh's NEET PG exam

 

The Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Arunachal Pradesh, conducts the NEET PG Ayush counseling in Arunachal Pradesh. There are two rounds to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Arunachal Pradesh state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Arunachal Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Arunachal Pradesh website is used for the online application procedure for NEET PG Ayush counseling in Arunachal Pradesh. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the application form for Arunachal Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling will be made accessible online by the Department of Health and Family Welfare of the Government of Arunachal Pradesh.

 

For the Arunachal Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling, aspirants must pay an application fee of INR 1,800 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST students. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for the Arunachal Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be made available on the website of the Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Arunachal Pradesh.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Arunachal Pradesh, will conduct an online counseling procedure to assign seats for the Arunachal Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Pradesh, Arunachal Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

Seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Arunachal Pradesh are distributed fairly and openly via NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, since there are only a limited number of postgraduate Ayush seats available in Arunachal Pradesh, applicants may need to make compromises about their preferred area of study or relocate to another state to complete their postgraduate Ayush studies.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 50 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Arunachal Pradesh.

Every year, a different cut-off score is used for Arunachal Pradesh's NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

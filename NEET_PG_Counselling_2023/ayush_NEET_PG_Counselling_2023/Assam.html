A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG by Ayush Counselling Assam

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Assam

 

The Directorate of Ayush, Assam, conducts the Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. Two rounds of counseling are conducted: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for Assam state quota seats.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The Directorate of Ayush, Assam website is used for the online application procedure for Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the Assam Directorate of Ayush website will host the application form for Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling.

 

For Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling, the application fee is 1,800 INR for general/OBC students and 1,200 INR for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. After the NEET PG Ayush test is completed, the merit list will be made available on the Directorate of Ayush, Assam website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Ayush, Assam will conduct an online counseling procedure to provide seats for the Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Assam Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Assam is via Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, since Assam only has a small number of postgraduate Ayush seats available, applicants may need to make compromises on their specialized choice or go to another state to complete their postgraduate Ayush study.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Assam.

Each year, a different cut-off is used for Assam NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

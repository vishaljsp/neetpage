Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Chhattisgarh

 

The Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CGPEB) oversees the Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling. There are two stages to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Chhattisgarh state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The CGPEB website is used to submit applications for Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling application form will be accessible on the CGPEB website.

 

For Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling, the application cost is INR 1,800 for general/OBC students and INR 1,200 for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the CGPEB website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The CGPEB's online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for the Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling session. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

NEET PG Ayush counseling for Chhattisgarh in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Chhattisgarh is via the NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, because to a lack of postgraduate Ayush seats in Chhattisgarh, applicants may be forced to make compromises on their chosen expertise or go to another state to complete their study.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Chhattisgarh.

The qualifying score for Chhattisgarh NEET PG Ayush counseling changes every year. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

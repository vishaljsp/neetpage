The Admission Process in NEET PG Ayush Counselling Haryana

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), an entrance test for postgraduate medical programs at the national level in India. For entry into postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, there is a special test called NEET Ayush.

 

Ayush Counseling for Haryana NEET PG

 

The Haryana State Medical Education and Research Institute (HSMERI) oversees the counseling for the Haryana NEET PG Ayush exam. The counseling procedure is separated into two rounds: the first phase is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ); the second round is for seats under the Haryana state quota.

 

Criteria for Eligibility

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

The NEET PG Ayush test must have been cleared by them.

They needed to have earned a minimum of 50% overall (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

They need to be medical graduates and have finished a year of internship.

They have to possess a current NEET PG Ayush score.

Process of Applying

 

Through the HSMERI website, applicants may submit their applications for Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the application form for Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling will be accessible on the HSMERI website.

 

For the Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling, aspirants must pay an application fee of INR 1,800 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST students. You may use debit, credit, or net banking to pay the application cost online.

 

Honor Roll

 

Based on the candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and selected speciality, a merit list for Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling would be created. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the HSMERI website.

 

Seat Allocation

 

The HSMERI will conduct an online counseling procedure to provide seats for the Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling. Based on the applicants' NEET PG Ayush scores, specialty preferences, and category, seats in postgraduate Ayush programs will be assigned.

 

Essential Dates

 

Exam date for the 2023 NEET PG Ayush: September 9

20 October 2023: NEET PG Ayush 2023 outcome

Haryana NEET Post Graduate Ayush Counseling 2023: November 2023

 

Seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Haryana are distributed fairly and transparently via the Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling process. However, given the scarcity of postgraduate Ayush seats in Haryana, students would be forced to make compromises regarding their specialized choices or relocate to other states to complete their postgraduate Ayush studies.

 

Informational Supplement

 

In Haryana, there are around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats available.

Each year, a different cut-off score is used for Haryana NEET PG Ayush counseling. But 400 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

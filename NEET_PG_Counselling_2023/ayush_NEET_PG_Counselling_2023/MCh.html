A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

After completing a three-year residency program in a surgical speciality, candidates are given the M.CH., or Master of Surgery, postgraduate medical degree. There are many specialties available for the M.CH program in Ayush, including Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Tasawwuf.

 

Candidates are assigned seats in the M.CH Ayush programs provided by different medical institutions in India via the NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH procedure. The Directorate General of Ayush (DG Ayush) oversees the counseling process.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH.

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They must have earned their M.D. in Ayush from a reputable institution.

They must have completed a 12-month internship at an accredited medical school.

They must have successfully completed a one-year, required, rotating internship at a licensed medical school.

Application Method

 

The DG Ayush website is used to submit applications for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH 2023 application form will be accessible on the DG Ayush website.

 

For NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH applicants, the cost is INR 1,800 for general/OBC applicants and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be made available on the DG Ayush website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The DG Ayush will conduct online counseling to assign seats for the NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH program. In accordance with the applicants' NEET PG Ayush scores, specialty preferences, and category, seats in M.CH Ayush programs will be assigned to them.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in M.CH Ayush programs in India is via the NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH. Due to the lack of M.CH Ayush seats in India, applicants may have to make compromises regarding their specialized choice or may need to go abroad to complete their M.CH Ayush education.

 

Additional Details

 

There are around 500 M.CH Ayush seats in India.

Annual changes are made to the NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.CH cut-off. However, 700 is often considered the cutoff.

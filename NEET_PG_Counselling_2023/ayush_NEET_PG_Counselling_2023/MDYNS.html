The Admissions Process for NEET PG Ayush Counselling MD Yoga and Naturopathy

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including MD Yoga and Naturopathy, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD yoga and naturopathy:

 

They must have successfully completed the B.Y.S.M.S./B.N.Y.S.M. test at an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) website is used to submit applications for NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD Yoga and Naturopathy. From March 1 through April 30, 2023, the NEET PG Ayush 2023 application form will be accessible on the NBME website.

 

For NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD Yoga and Naturopathy, aspirants must pay an application fee of INR 1,800 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD Yoga and Naturopathy. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the NBME website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The online counseling procedure run by the NBME will be used to allocate seats for NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD Yoga and Naturopathy. A spot in a postgraduate yoga and naturopathy program will be given to each applicant depending on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate yoga and naturopathy programs is via NEET PG Ayush counseling in MD Yoga and Naturopathy. To pursue their postgraduate Yoga and Naturopathy education, students may have to make compromises on their chosen expertise or go to other states owing to the lack of postgraduate Yoga and Naturopathy seats in India.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 places are available for postgraduate study in yoga and naturopathy in India.

The threshold for MD Yoga and Naturopathy NEET PG Ayush counseling changes from year to year. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

There are two phases to the NBME counseling procedure for NEET PG Ayush. The All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the first round, while the state quota seats are determined in the second round.

A Guide to the Admission Procedure for NEET PG Ayush Counselling MS Unani

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including MS Unani, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be satisfied by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET PG Ayush counseling at MS Unani:

 

They must have achieved success on the BUMS/BHMS/BUMS test from an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) website is used to submit applications for NEET PG Ayush counseling in MS Unani. From March 1 through April 30, 2023, the NEET PG Ayush 2023 application form will be accessible on the NBME website.

 

For NEET PG Ayush counseling at MS Unani, the application fee is INR 1,800 for general/OBC students and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG Ayush counseling at MS Unani. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the NBME website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The online counseling procedure run by the NBME will be used to allocate seats for NEET PG Ayush counseling at MS Unani. Based on the applicants' NEET PG Ayush scores, specialty preferences, and category, seats in postgraduate Unani programs will be assigned.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Unani programs is via NEET PG Ayush counseling in MS Unani. To pursue their postgraduate Unani education, aspirants may have to make compromises on their speciality choice or go to other states owing to the restricted number of postgraduate Unani seats in India.

 

Additional Details

 

In India, there are around 500 postgraduate Unani seats available.

The cut-off for MS Unani's NEET PG Ayush counseling changes every year. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

There are two phases to the NBME counseling procedure for NEET PG Ayush. The All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the first round, while the state quota seats are determined in the second round.

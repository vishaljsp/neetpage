Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

The NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Madhya Pradesh

 

The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPPEB) manages the Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. There are two rounds to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Madhya Pradesh state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The MPPEB website is used to submit applications for the Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling procedure. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling application form will be accessible on the MPPEB website.

 

Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counselling applicants must pay an application fee of INR 1,800 for general/OBC applicants and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for the Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the MPPEB website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The MPPEB's online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for the Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Pradesh, Madhya Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Madhya Pradesh is via NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, given the lack of postgraduate Ayush seats in Madhya Pradesh, applicants may be forced to make compromises on their preferred area of study or relocate to another state to complete their postgraduate Ayush education.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Madhya Pradesh.

Every year, a different cut-off score is used for Madhya Pradesh NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Counselling in Meghalaya

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Meghalaya

 

The Government of Meghalaya's Directorate of Health Services administers the Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling. There are two stages to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Meghalaya state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The Directorate of Health Services, Government of Meghalaya website is used to submit applications for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the Meghalaya Directorate of Health Services website will host the application form for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling.

 

For Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush Counseling, the application fee is INR 1,800 for General/OBC students and INR 1,200 for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be made available on the website of the Directorate of Health Services, Government of Meghalaya.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Health Services, Government of Meghalaya, will conduct an online counseling procedure to provide seats for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Counseling for Meghalaya's NEET PG in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Meghalaya is via the use of NEET PG Ayush counseling. Candidates may have to make compromises regarding their choice of speciality or may need to travel to other states in order to complete their postgraduate Ayush study owing to the low number of postgraduate Ayush seats in Meghalaya.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 50 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Meghalaya.

Each year, a different cut-off score is used for Meghalaya NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

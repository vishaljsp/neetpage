A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Ayush Counselling in Mizoram

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Mizoram

 

The Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Mizoram, provides NEET PG Ayush counseling in Mizoram. There are two stages to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Mizoram state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

Online applications are accepted via the website of the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Mizoram, for Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the application form for Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling will be made accessible online by the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Mizoram.

 

For Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling, the application fee is 1,800 INR for general/OBC students and 1,200 INR for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be made available on the website of the Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Mizoram.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Mizoram, will conduct an online counseling procedure to provide seats for the Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Counseling for Mizoram NEET PG in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Mizoram is via NEET PG Ayush counseling. Candidates may, however, be forced to make compromises regarding their choice of speciality or may need to travel to other states in order to complete their postgraduate Ayush study owing to the restricted number of postgraduate Ayush seats in Mizoram.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 50 Ayush postgraduate places are available in Mizoram.

The qualifying score for Mizoram NEET PG Ayush counseling changes every year. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

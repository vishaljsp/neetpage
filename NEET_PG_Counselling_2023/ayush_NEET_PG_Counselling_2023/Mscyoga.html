A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Ayush Counselling M.Sc. Yoga

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be qualified for NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. yoga:

 

They must have successfully completed the B.A.M.S./B.N.Y.S.M. test at an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) website is used to submit applications for NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. yoga. From March 1 through April 30, 2023, the NEET PG Ayush 2023 application form will be accessible on the NBME website.

 

For NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. Yoga, the application fee is INR 1,800 for general/OBC students and INR 1,200 for SC/ST applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. Yoga. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the NBME website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The online counseling method run by the NBME will be used to allocate seats for NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. Yoga. A spot in a postgraduate yoga program will be given to each applicant based on their NEET PG Ayush score, speciality preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

Seat distribution for postgraduate yoga programs is clear and equitable thanks to NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. yoga. To pursue their postgraduate Yoga education, aspirants may have to make compromises on their chosen expertise or go to other states owing to the restricted number of postgraduate Yoga seats in India.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 people study yoga as a postgraduate in India.

The minimum score required for NEET PG Ayush counseling in M.Sc. Yoga changes every year. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

There are two phases to the NBME counseling procedure for NEET PG Ayush. The All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the first round, while the state quota seats are determined in the second round.

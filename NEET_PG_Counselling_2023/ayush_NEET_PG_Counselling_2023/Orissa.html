A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG in Odisha

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Odisha

 

The Odisha Joint Entrance Examinations Board (OJEEB) manages the Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling. There are two stages to the counseling process: the first phase is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Odisha state quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following prerequisites must be satisfied by applicants in order to be eligible for Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

Through the OJEEB website, applicants for Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling submit their applications. From March 1 to April 30, 2023, the application form for Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling will be accessible on the OJEEB website.

 

For the Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling, aspirants must pay an application fee of INR 1,800 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,200 for SC/ST students. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the OJEEB website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The OJEEB's online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for the Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling session. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Odisha Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Odisha is via the NEET PG Ayush counseling. Although there are only a limited number of postgraduate Ayush seats available in Odisha, applicants may be forced to compromise on their preferred area of study or relocate to another state to complete their postgraduate Ayush education.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Odisha.

Each year, a different cut-off is used for the Odisha NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

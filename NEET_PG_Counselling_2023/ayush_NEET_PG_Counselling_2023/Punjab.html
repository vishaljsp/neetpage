A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Counselling in Punjab

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to postgraduate Ayush programs including M.Sc. Yoga, MD Homeopathy, and MD Naturopathy, candidates must pass the NEET Ayush test.

 

NEET PG Ayush Counseling in Punjab

 

The Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot, offers Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. There are two stages to the counseling process: the first round is for seats under the All India Quota (AIQ), and the second round is for seats under the Punjab State Quota.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling:

 

They have to be NEET PG Ayush exam winners.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

A valid NEET PG Ayush score is required.

Application Method

 

The BFUHS website is used for the online application procedure for Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. Between March 1 and April 30, 2023, the application form for Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling will be accessible on the BFUHS website.

 

General/OBC applicants must pay INR 1,800 as their application fee for the Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling, while SC/ST candidates must pay INR 1,200. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG Ayush score and specialty preference will be used to create the merit list for Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET PG Ayush test, the merit list will be posted on the BFUHS website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The BFUHS's online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for the Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. A candidate's seat in a postgraduate Ayush program will be assigned based on their NEET PG Ayush score, specialty preference, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for NEET PG Ayush 2023: September 9, 2023

Results of the NEET PG Ayush 2023: October 20, 2023

Punjab Conclusion for NEET PG Ayush counseling in 2023: November 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in postgraduate Ayush programs in Punjab is via Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. Due to the shortage of postgraduate Ayush seats in Punjab, applicants may, nevertheless, be forced to make compromises regarding their specialized choices or to relocate to other states in order to complete their postgraduate Ayush studies.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 postgraduate Ayush seats are available in Punjab.

Each year, a different cut-off score is used for Punjab NEET PG Ayush counseling. However, 400 is often considered the cutoff.

A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET PG Counselling

 

The National Board of Examinations (NBME) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level test for admission to postgraduate medical programs in India. For admission to MD/MS/Diploma/DM/MCh programs in government, private, deemed universities, and private medical schools in India, NEET PG is a single window test.

 

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates need to satisfy the following requirements in order to be eligible for NEET PG counseling for MD:

 

They must have succeeded on the MBBS/BDS test from an accredited institution.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As a medical graduate, they must have completed a year of internship.

Application Method

 

The NBME website is used to submit applications for NEET PG counseling for MD students. Between March 1 and April 30, 2023, the NEET PG 2023 application form will be made accessible on the NBME website.

 

For general/OBC applicants and SC/ST candidates, the application cost for NEET PG counseling for MD is INR 1,800 and INR 1,200, respectively. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET PG score will be used to create the merit list for NEET PG counseling for MD. After the NEET PG test is over, the merit list will be made available on the NBME website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The NBME online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for NEET PG counseling for MD students. Based on the applicants' NEET PG scores, preferred colleges, and category, seats in an MD program at an Indian medical institution would be assigned.

 

Significant Dates

 

Exam date for the 2023 NEET PG: September 9

Result of NEET PG 2023: October 20, 2023

NEET PG guidance MD November 2023 2023: 2023: End

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in MD programs at medical schools in India is via NEET PG counseling for MD. If you want to work as a doctor in a specific area, you must pass the NEET PG counseling for MD test.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 25,000 MD positions are available in India.

The minimum score required for MD NEET PG counseling changes from year to year. But 600 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two stages to the NBME counseling procedure for MD. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

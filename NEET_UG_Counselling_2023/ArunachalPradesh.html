Arunachal Pradesh's NEET UG Admissions Process: A Guide

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is the only entrance test for MBBS and BDS admission in Arunachal Pradesh.

 

The Directorate of Higher & Technical Education, Arunachal Pradesh (DoTE) oversees the NEET counseling procedure in Arunachal Pradesh. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online. Only if seats remain open after the first two rounds will there be a third.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates in Arunachal Pradesh who want to participate in NEET UG must fulfill the requirements listed below:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

Their primary residence must be in Arunachal Pradesh.

Application Method

 

In Arunachal Pradesh, the NTA website is used for the online NEET UG application procedure. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

In Arunachal Pradesh, the NEET UG application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET score will be used to create the merit list for NEET UG in Arunachal Pradesh. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The DoTE Arunachal Pradesh online counseling procedure will be used to provide seats for NEET UG in that state. Based on the applicants' NEET scores, college preferences, and category, seats in medical colleges will be assigned.

The entrance to a medical profession is via NEET UG. The Arunachal Pradesh NEET counseling procedure is a fair and transparent mechanism to distribute seats among the state's medical schools. NEET UG is the test you must pass if you want to pursue a career as a doctor.

 

Additional Details

 

In Arunachal Pradesh, there are 400 MBBS seats and 100 BDS seats, respectively.

The Arunachal Pradesh NEET UG cut-off fluctuates from year to year. However, 550 is generally considered to be the cutoff for MBBS.

There are two rounds to the Arunachal Pradesh NEET counseling procedure. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

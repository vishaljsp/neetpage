NEET UG Bihar: Everything You Need to Know

 

There is a national entrance exam called the countrywide Eligibility cum admission exam (NEET) for undergraduate medical and dental courses in India. The exam is delivered by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

 

For NEET UG, Bihar is offering a total of 1,500 MBBS seats and 600 BDS seats. These seats are available at 13 medical schools and 3 dental institutions in the state.

 

The NEET UG counselling process in Bihar is managed by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB), located in Patna. The counselling procedure is divided into two phases:

 

First round: This phase is open to candidates who choose the 85% state quota seats.

stage 2: This stage is open to applicants who choose one of the 15% All India Quota (AIQ) seats.

Seats are distributed in both rounds based on applicants' merit rankings and college choices.

 

NEET UG Eligibility Requirements in Bihar

 

For candidates to be eligible to apply for NEET UG in Bihar, they must meet the criteria given below:

 

They must be a citizen of India.

They must have earned a passing grade on a Class 12 exam in Physics, Chemistry, or Biology, or a test with a similar focus.

They must get a combined grade in physics, chemistry, and biology (PCB) of at least 50%.

The NEET UG upper age limit is 25 years old as of the exam date.

The NEET UG Application Process in Bihar

 

The NEET UG application process in Bihar is done online. Utilising the official website of the NTA is how to apply. The application process runs from April to May.

 

The NEET UG application fee in Bihar is Rs. 1400 for candidates in the general category and Rs. 750 for those in the SC/ST/PwD categories.

 

Date & time of the NEET UG exam in Bihar

 

The NEET UG exam for Bihar will be held on May 7, 2023. For the test, there will be two shifts:

 

First shift is from 10 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. Second shift is from 2:30 to 5:00

The exam will be given at many testing centres spread out over the state.

 

NEET UG results for Bihar

 

The NEET UG results for Bihar will be announced in June 2023. The results will be posted on the NTA's official website.

 

NEET UG Counselling in Bihar

 

Bihar will start offering NEET UG counselling in July 2023. There will be online treatment sessions.

 

Those who cleared the NEET UG exam will get a counselling invitation. Throughout the counselling procedure, candidates will be awarded spots in the state's medical and dental schools based on their merit standing and college choices.

Everything You Need to Know About NEET UG Punjab

 

For undergraduate medical and dentistry programmes in India, there is a nationwide admission test called the nationwide Eligibility cum admission Test (NEET). The National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the test.

 

There are a total of 1425 MBBS seats and 1260 BDS seats open for NEET UG in Punjab. 11 medical schools and 6 dentistry schools in the state are offering these seats.

 

The Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot, oversees the NEET UG counselling procedure for Punjab. There are three stages to the counselling process:

 

Round 1: Candidates who selected the 85% state quota seats are eligible to run in this round.

Round 2: Candidates who selected the 15% All India Quota (AIQ) seats are eligible for this round.

Round 3: These seats from Rounds 1 and 2 will be filled in this round.

Based on the applicants' merit rankings and college preferences, seats are distributed according to each round's candidates.

 

Punjabi NEET UG Eligibility Requirements

 

Candidates must fulfil the requirements listed below in order to be eligible to apply for NEET UG in Punjab:

 

They must be an Indian national.

They must have passed a test in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, or an equivalent exam in Class 12 or a related topic.

They must get a combined grade of at least 50% in physics, chemistry, and biology (PCB).

As of the exam date, the NEET UG upper age restriction is 25 years old.

Punjab's NEET UG Application Process

 

In Punjab, the NEET UG application procedure is conducted online. Applying is done via the NTA's official website. Beginning in April and ending in May is the application procedure.

 

Punjab's NEET UG application fees are Rs. 1400 for general category applicants and Rs. 750 for SC/ST/PwD applicants.

 

Punjab NEET UG Exam Date and Time

 

Punjab's NEET UG test will take place on May 7, 2023. There will be two shifts for the exam:

 

10 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. on shift one

Second shift: 2:30 to 5:00

A number of testing locations around the state will host the exam.

 

Punjab NEET UG Results

 

Punjab's NEET UG results will be released in June 2023. The NTA's official website will host the outcome.

 

Punjab NEET UG Counselling

 

Beginning in July 2023, Punjab will provide NEET UG counselling. Online therapy sessions will be held.

 

Those who passed the NEET UG test will get an invitation for counselling. Candidates will be assigned places in the state's medical and dentistry institutions throughout the counselling process based on their merit standing and college preferences.

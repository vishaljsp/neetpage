A Guide to the Sikkim NEET UG Admissions Process

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is the only entrance test Sikkim uses to admit students to MBBS and BDS programs.

 

The Sikkim Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE) oversees the Sikkim NEET counseling procedure. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online. Only in the event that there are still open seats after the second round would the third round be held.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be met by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET UG in Sikkim:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

They need to reside in Sikkim.

Application Method

 

Sikkim's NEET UG application procedure is completed online at the NTA website. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

Sikkim's NEET UG application costs INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET score will be used to create the merit list for NEET UG in Sikkim. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The DoTE Sikkim online counseling procedure will be used to allocate seats for NEET UG in Sikkim. Based on the applicants' NEET scores, college preferences, and category, seats in medical colleges will be assigned.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

NEET counseling in Sikkim in 2023: July 2023

Conclusion

 

The entrance to a medical profession is via NEET UG. The Sikkim NEET counseling procedure is a fair and transparent method of distributing seats across the state's medical schools. NEET UG is the test you must pass if you want to pursue a career as a doctor.

 

Additional Details

 

Sikkim has 250 places for MBBS and 50 seats for BDS, respectively.

The Sikkim NEET UG cut-off varies from year to year. However, 600 is generally considered to be the cutoff for MBBS.

There are two rounds to the Sikkim NEET counseling procedure. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

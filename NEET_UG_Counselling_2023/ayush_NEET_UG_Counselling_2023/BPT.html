UG NEET A Guide to the Admissions Process for Ayush Counseling

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also utilized for admission to BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy), in addition to MBBS and BDS.

 

The Directorate of Ayush, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, manages the Ayush NEET counseling process for B.P.T. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be satisfied by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counseling for B.P.T.

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

Application Method

 

The NTA website is used for the online application procedure for NEET UG Ayush counseling for B.P.T. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For NEET UG Ayush counseling for B.P.T. applicants, the application fee is INR 2,000 for general/OBC candidates and INR 1,500 for SC/ST candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The NEET UG merit list The NEET score of the applicant will be used to prepare Ayush counseling for B.P.T. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Ayush will use its online counseling system to provide seats for NEET UG Ayush counseling for B.P.T. at India, seats at B.P.T. colleges will be assigned to applicants based on their NEET scores, college preferences, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

B.P.T. for Ayush NEET counseling in 2023: July

 

Seat distribution in B.P.T. institutions in India is done in a transparent and equitable manner using NEET UG Ayush counseling. In order to become a physiotherapist in India, you must pass the NEET UG Ayush counseling for B.P.T exam.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 20,000 B.P.T. seats are available in India.

The cut-off for BPT NEET UG Ayush counseling changes every year. But 550 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two phases to the Ayush NEET counseling procedure for BPT students. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

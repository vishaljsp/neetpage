For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also used for admission to the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS), Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS), and Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy, and Siddha (BYNS) programs in addition to the MBBS and BDS.

 

The Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), which is located in Punjab, manages the Ayush NEET counseling program. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be satisfied by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counseling in Punjab:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

They need to reside in Punjab.

Application Method

 

Online applications are accepted via the NTA website for Punjab's NEET UG Ayush counseling. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For NEET UG Ayush counseling in Punjab, the application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

a seat assignment

 

The BFUHS online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for NEET UG Ayush counseling in Punjab. Depending on the NEET score, the candidate's preferred college, and their category, a seat in a BAMS/BHMS/BYNS college in Punjab will be assigned.

NEET UG Ayush Counselling Jharkhand: A Guide to the Admission Process

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level medical entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses in India. In addition to MBBS and BDS, NEET is also used for admission to BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery), BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery), and BYNS (Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy and Siddha).

The Ayush NEET counselling process in Jharkhand is conducted by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB). The counselling is held in two rounds, both of which are conducted online.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand, candidates must meet the following criteria:

They must have passed the 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as compulsory subjects.

They must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate (40% for SC/ST candidates and 45% for PwD candidates).

They must have completed 17 years of age as of December 31, 2023.

They must be domiciled in Jharkhand.

Application Process

The application process for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand is conducted online through the NTA website. The application form for NEET UG 2023 will be available on the NTA website from April 1 to May 6, 2023.

The application fee for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand is INR 2,000 for General/OBC candidates and INR 1,500 for SC/ST candidates. The application fee can be paid online using Debit/Credit/Net banking.

Merit List

The merit list for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand will be prepared on the basis of the candidate's NEET score. The merit list will be published on the NTA website after the NEET exam is conducted.

Seat Allotment

The seat allotment for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand will be done through the JCECEB online counselling process. The candidates will be allotted a seat in a BAMS/BHMS/BYNS college in Jharkhand based on their NEET score, choice of college, and category.

Important Dates

NEET UG 2023 exam: May 7, 2023

NEET UG 2023 result: June 13, 2023

Ayush NEET counselling Jharkhand 2023: July 2023

Conclusion

NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand is a transparent and fair way to allocate seats in BAMS/BHMS/BYNS colleges in Jharkhand. If you are aspiring to become an Ayurvedic, homeopathic, or siddha doctor in Jharkhand, then NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand is the exam you need to clear.

Additional Information

The number of BAMS/BHMS/BYNS seats in Jharkhand is around 400.

The cut-off for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Jharkhand varies from year to year. However, the general cut-off is around 550.

The Ayush NEET counselling process in Jharkhand is divided into two rounds. The first round is for the state quota seats, and the second round is for the All India Quota (AIQ) seats.

The third round of counselling is held only if seats are left vacant after the first two rounds.

Madhya Pradesh NEET UG Ayush Counselling: A Guide to the Admission Procedure

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also used for admission to the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS), Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS), and Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy, and Siddha (BYNS) programs in addition to the MBBS and BDS.

 

 

The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Madhya Pradesh, is in charge of managing the Ayush NEET counseling procedure there. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must fulfill the requirements listed below in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counseling in Madhya Pradesh:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

They must have a Madhya Pradesh residence.

Application Method

 

Online applications are accepted via the NTA website for Madhya Pradesh's NEET UG Ayush counseling. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For Madhya Pradesh's NEET UG Ayush counseling, the application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET score will be used to create the merit list for Madhya Pradesh's NEET UG Ayush counseling. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The DME online counseling mechanism will be used to allocate seats for the NEET UG Ayush counseling in Madhya Pradesh. Based on the applicants' NEET scores, preferred colleges, and category, seats in Madhya Pradesh's BAMS/BHMS/BYNS colleges will be assigned.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

NEET guidance from Ayush in Madhya Pradesh July 2023: End of the year 2023

 

Madhya Pradesh's NEET UG Ayush counseling is a transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in BAMS/BHMS/BYNS institutions. NEET UG Ayush Counseling in Madhya Pradesh is the test you must pass if you want to practice Ayurveda, Homeopathy, or Siddha medicine in Madhya Pradesh.

 

Additional Details

 

There are around 500 BAMS/BHMS/BYNS seats available in Madhya Pradesh.

The cut-off for Madhya Pradesh's NEET UG Ayush counseling changes every year. But 550 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two phases to the Ayush NEET counseling procedure in Madhya Pradesh. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

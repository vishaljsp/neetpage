NEET UG Ayush Counselling Meghalaya: A Guide to the Admission Process

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level medical entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses in India. In addition to MBBS and BDS, NEET is also used for admission to BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery), BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery), and BYNS (Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy and Siddha).

The Ayush NEET counselling process in Meghalaya is conducted by the Directorate of Ayush, Meghalaya. The counselling is held in two rounds, both of which are conducted online.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • They must have passed the 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as compulsory subjects.
  • They must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate (40% for SC/ST candidates and 45% for PwD candidates).
  • They must have completed 17 years of age as of December 31, 2023.
  • They must be domiciled in Meghalaya.

Application Process

The application process for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya is conducted online through the NTA website. The application form for NEET UG 2023 will be available on the NTA website from April 1 to May 6, 2023.

The application fee for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya is INR 2,000 for General/OBC candidates and INR 1,500 for SC/ST candidates. The application fee can be paid online using Debit/Credit/Net banking.

Merit List

The merit list for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya will be prepared on the basis of the candidate's NEET score. The merit list will be published on the NTA website after the NEET exam is conducted.

Seat Allotment

The seat allotment for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya will be done through the Directorate of Ayush online counselling process. The candidates will be allotted a seat in a BAMS/BHMS/BYNS college in Meghalaya based on their NEET score, choice of college, and category.

Important Dates

  • NEET UG 2023 exam: May 7, 2023
  • NEET UG 2023 result: June 13, 2023
  • Ayush NEET counselling Meghalaya 2023: July 2023

NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya is a transparent and fair way to allocate seats in BAMS/BHMS/BYNS colleges in Meghalaya. If you are aspiring to become an Ayurvedic, homeopathic, or siddha doctor in Meghalaya, then NEET UG Ayush counselling in Meghalaya is the exam you need to clear.

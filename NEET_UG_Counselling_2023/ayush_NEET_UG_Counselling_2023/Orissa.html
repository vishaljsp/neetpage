A Guide to the Admission Procedure for NEET UG Ayush Counselling in Odisha

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programmes in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also used for admission to the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS), Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS), and Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy, and Siddha (BYNS) programmes in addition to the MBBS and BDS.

 

The Odisha State Council of Higher Education (OSCE) oversees the Ayush NEET counselling procedure in that state. Two rounds of counselling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

Candidates must satisfy the requirements below in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Odisha:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

They need to reside in Odisha.

Application Method

 

Through the NTA website, applications are accepted for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Odisha. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For NEET UG Ayush counselling in Odisha, the application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST students and INR 2,000 for general/OBC candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The NEET UG merit list The candidate's NEET score will be used to prepare the Ayush counselling in Odisha. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The OSCE online counselling procedure will be used to distribute seats for NEET UG Ayush counselling in Odisha. In Odisha, seats in BAMS, BHMS, and BYNS colleges will be assigned to students based on their NEET scores, college preferences, and category.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

Odisha NEET counselling by Ayush 2023: July 2023

 

Seat distribution in BAMS/BHMS/BYNS institutions in Odisha is done in a transparent and equitable manner via NEET UG Ayush counselling. You must pass the NEET UG Ayush counselling in Odisha test if you want to practise as an Ayurvedic, homoeopathic, or siddha doctor in that state.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 500 seats in BAMS, BHMS, and BYNS are available in Odisha.

The cut-off for Odisha's NEET UG Ayush counselling changes every year. But 550 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two phases to the Ayush NEET counselling procedure in Odisha. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counselling are there held the third round.

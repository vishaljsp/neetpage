A Guide to the Admission Procedure for NEET UG Counselling in Sikkim

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also used for admission to the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS), Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS), and Bachelor of Yoga, Naturopathy, and Siddha (BYNS) programs in addition to the MBBS and BDS.

 

 

The Directorate of Ayush, Sikkim, oversees the Sikkim NEET counseling procedure. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be met by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counseling in Sikkim:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

They need to reside in Sikkim.

Application Method

 

Online applications are accepted via the NTA website for Sikkim's NEET UG Ayush counseling. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For Sikkim's NEET UG Ayush counseling, the application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC applicants. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The NEET UG merit list The candidate's NEET score will be used to create the Sikkim Ayush counseling plan. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The Directorate of Ayush will use its online counseling system to allocate seats for NEET UG Ayush counseling in Sikkim. Based on the students' NEET scores, college preferences, and category, seats would be assigned to BAMS/BHMS/BYNS colleges in Sikkim.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

NEET counseling from Ayush in Sikkim in 2023: July 2023

 

Sikkim's NEET UG Ayush counseling is a fair and open means to distribute seats among BAMS, BHMS, and BYNS institutions. NEET UG Ayush Counseling in Sikkim is the test you must pass if you want to work as an Ayurvedic, homeopathic, or siddha doctor in Sikkim.

 

Additional Details

 

About 200 people are enrolled in BAMS, BHMS, and BYNS programs in Sikkim.

The cut-off for Sikkim's NEET UG Ayush counseling changes every year. But 550 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

Sikkim's Ayush NEET counseling procedure consists of two phases. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

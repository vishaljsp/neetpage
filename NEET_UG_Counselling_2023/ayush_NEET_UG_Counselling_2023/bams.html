A Guide to the Admission Process for NEET UG Ayush Counselling BAMS

 

For admission to undergraduate medical and dentistry programs in India, the National Testing Agency (NTA) administers the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), a national-level medical entrance test. NEET is also utilized for admission to BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery) and other Ayush programmes in addition to MBBS and BDS.

 

The Ayush Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC) manages the Ayush NEET counseling process for admission to BAMS and other Ayush programs. Two rounds of counseling are undertaken, both of which are done online.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following requirements must be satisfied by applicants in order to be eligible for NEET UG Ayush counseling BAMS:

 

They must have completed the 10+2 exam with a minimum of passing grades in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

They had to have received an overall grade of at least 50% (45% for candidates with disabilities and 40% for SC/ST candidates).

As on December 31, 2023, they must be at least 17 years old.

Application Method

 

The NTA website is used to submit applications for NEET UG Ayush counseling and BAMS. Between April 1 and May 6, 2023, the NTA website will host the application for NEET UG.

 

For NEET UG Ayush counseling BAMS, the application fee is INR 1,500 for SC/ST applicants and INR 2,000 for general/OBC candidates. Online payment options for the application fee include debit, credit, and net banking.

 

Award List

 

The candidate's NEET score will be used to create the merit list for NEET UG Ayush counseling BAMS. Following the NEET test, the merit list will be posted on the NTA website.

 

a seat assignment

 

The AACCC online counseling procedure will be used to allocate seats for NEET UG Ayush counseling BAMS. Based on the applicants' NEET scores, college preferences, and category, seats at BAMS colleges will be assigned to them.

 

Significant Dates

 

May 7, 2023: NEET UG exam

June 13, 2023: NEET UG 2023 results

NEET counseling for Ayush in 2023: July 2023

 

A transparent and equitable method of allocating seats in BAMS institutions in India is via the NEET UG Ayush counseling. If you want to work as an ayurvedic physician, you must pass the NEET UG Ayush Counseling BAMS test.

 

Additional Details

 

Around 14,000 BAMS seats are available in India.

The threshold for NEET UG Ayush counseling and BAMS changes every year. But 600 is generally considered to be the cutoff.

There are two phases to the Ayush NEET counseling procedure. State quota seats are determined in the first round, while All India Quota (AIQ) seats are determined in the second round.

Only when seats remain open after the first two rounds of counseling are there held the third round.

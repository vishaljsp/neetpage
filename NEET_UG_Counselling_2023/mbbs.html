MBBS Counselling 2023: Registration (Date Announced), Important dates, Counselling process, Participating Colleges, and, More


NEET-UG 2023 Counselling for AIQ seats is going to be commenced on July 20th, 2023. MCC i.e. Medical Counselling Committee has just released the schedule for All India Quota MBBS Counselling 2023. MCC is the sole authority to conduct MBBS/BDS/B.Sc. Nursing Counselling for All India Quota Counselling 2023. 


NEET 2023 is the sole entrance exam to get admission to medical/dental/nursing colleges. Aspirants need to qualify for NEET-UG 2023 followed by the counselling conducted by MCC New Delhi to get admission to any undergraduate program facilitated by different colleges across India. 


NEET-UG 2023 Counselling is conducted every year in online mode. Candidates can visit https://mcc.nic.in for more details. In the following article, you will get to know more on the counselling process, participating institutes, schedule, and more.

  • 100% MBBS Seats in JIPMER (Puducherry/Karaikal)

As per the latest circular, none of any vacant/unclaimed seats shall be reverted to the respective state.

NEET-UG 2023 Counselling: Counselling Process


NEET-UG 2023 Counselling process is a pre defined sequence of events that will be going to take place from July 20th 2023. The entire process needs to be participated in for admission to the top medical/dental/nursing colleges across the country. Candidates need to visit https://mcc.nic.in for the latest notices.

Registration 2023:


  • Candidates need to register themselves at the official portal of MCC i.e. https://mcc.nic.in. 


  • Enter basic details like Name, DOB, Roll Number, NEET-UG 2023 application form number, and relevant details.


  • Login Credentials for NEET-UG 2023 Counselling will be generated.


  • After login, enter personal details like parent details, contact details, and category.


  • Enter NEET-UG 2023 details such as application number and Roll number.

Registration Fee 2023:


  • Candidates will be required to pay the registration fee according to the category that they hold. The registration fee for NEET-UG 2023 Counselling is non-refundable.


  • Candidates need to pay a security deposit as per their category requirements. Security deposit is refundable however in certain cases, deposits can be forfeited.


  • Registration fee for NEET-UG 2023 Counselling can only be paid via online mode. Candidates can pay registration fees through Debit/Credit cards and Internet Banking.

Choice filling and Locking 2023:


Choice filling and Locking lead to the final preferred choice of colleges and to attempt this course of Action, Candidates need to visit the MCC portal at https://mcc.nic.in. 


  • Login using the credentials i.e. Stream (Medical/Dental), Roll Number, and Password.


  • Candidates need to fill in choices of colleges for seat allotment.


  • The choices filled by MBBS aspirants need to be locked however if one forgets to lock the choices, it will be locked automatically.


  • Candidates are advised to take a printout of their filled choices for future reference.


  • The same steps are to be followed in the consecutive rounds of Counselling for NEET-UG 2023.

Seat Allotment 2023:


NEET-UG 2023 Counselling, seat allotment will be done on multiple factors like NEET-UG 2023 Rank, Choices of Colleges & Courses filled, availability of seats, and reservation criteria. 


MCC will release the NEET-UG 2023, seat allotment letters for shortlisted candidates on the official portal of MCC. After having the seat allotment letter, candidates will be required to report to the allotted college. More details can be obtained at https://mcc.nic.in. 

NEET-UG 2023 Counselling: Required Documents


From 2023 onwards, candidates need to upload the required list of documents to the MCC portal before reporting to the allotted college. This is an important step that every candidate needs to accomplish.


Original documents (along with attested copies of documents) for admission to Undergraduate medical/dental/nursing courses for admission to the allotted college are required at the time of joining. Essential documents that need to be brought up by the candidates are mentioned below. 


  1. Allotment letter issued by MCC

  2. Admit card issued by NTA 

  3. Result/Rank letter issued by NTA

  4. Date of Birth Certificate

  5. Class 10th Certificate

  6. Class 12th Certificate

  7. Class 12th Marksheet

  8. Eight Passport size Photographs same as affixed on the application form

  9. Proof of Identity (Aadhar/PAN/Driving Licence/Passport)

  10. Caste Certificate issued by the competent authority (If applicable)

  11.  Disability Certificate (PwD Candidates)

NEET-UG 2023 Counselling: Instructions for NRI Candidates 


Candidates falling under the NRI category as per the directions of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, are eligible for NRI Quota. As per the directions of MCC, 15% of total Undergraduate seats, are reserved for NRI Quota. 


The candidates, who wish to claim the NRI Quota seats will have to submit the following documents called for by the MCC. More details can be ascertained at https://mcc.nic.in.  


  • Documents claiming that the Sponsorer is an NRI (Passport, Visa of the Sponsorer)

  • Relationship of NRI with the Candidate

  • Duly Notarized Affidavit from the Sponsorer that he/she will sponsor the entire course fee of the candidate

  • Embassy certificate of the Sponsorer (Certificate from the Consulate)

  • NEET-UG 2023 Score card of the candidate
